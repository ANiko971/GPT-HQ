iPhone & iPad Setup for Offer Tracking

For offer tracking to work correctly, iOS must allow apps and websites to request tracking permissions.

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and turn on Allow Apps to Request to Track. When iOS asks whether an app can track your activity, choose Allow.

Safari’s privacy settings affect all browsers on iOS. These must be configured properly.

Open Settings → Safari and turn OFF:

  • Prevent Cross‑Site Tracking
  • Block All Cookies
  • Hide IP Address
  • Ask Websites Not to Track Me

Then go to Settings → Safari → Advanced and ensure Privacy Preserving Ad Measurement is enabled.

Some Wi‑Fi privacy features hide your IP address, which prevents tracking from working.

Go to Settings → Wi‑Fi → tap the (i) next to your network and disable:

  • Private Wi‑Fi Address
  • Limit IP Address Tracking

Private Relay hides your IP address and encrypts your traffic, which breaks offer tracking completely.

Go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → iCloud+ → Private Relay and switch it OFF.

Install Chrome from the App Store and set it as your default browser:

  • Open Settings → Chrome
  • Select Default Browser App → Chrome
  • Enable “Allow Cross‑Website Tracking”

When Chrome asks about cookies, choose Accept All.

Before beginning a new offer, clear your Chrome cookies:

  • Open Chrome → three dots
  • Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear Browsing Data
  • Select Cookies and Cached Images
  • Time range: All Time

Cookies are required for tracking. Accept cookies on:

  • The GPT site
  • Offer landing pages
  • Offerwall pages

If cookies are rejected, tracking breaks and you won’t receive credit.

When you click a mobile game offer, Chrome will redirect you to the App Store. This is how tracking is passed.

Never search for the game manually. Install it only through the offer link.

VPNs and ad‑blockers interfere with tracking. Turn off:

  • VPN apps
  • Ad‑blocker apps
  • Safari content blockers

Once a game is installed through an offer link, it contains a tracking ID. Removing the game erases this ID.

Avoid:

  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the game
  • Using “Offload App”
  • Switching Apple IDs
  • Resetting your device

Complete the offer requirements within the given timeframe (usually 30–90 days).
